## A Bit More

I believe that intelligence emerges from the interplay between a body, its sensors, and the world — not from computation alone.
That's what keeps me coming back to robotics: it's one of the few fields where you can't fake understanding.
The robot either picks up the cup, or it doesn't.

This blog is where I share paper notes, experiments, and reflections on the road toward smarter, more capable machines.

*Let's build something that actually works in the real world.* 🚀
